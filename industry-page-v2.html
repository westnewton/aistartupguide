Getting Started

New to AI? Here's where to start.

You don't need to adopt everything at once. Most contractors see the biggest impact from just one or two tools. Here's our recommended approach.

  • 1

    Start with your biggest time sink

    Spending 5 hours a week on estimates? Start with Joist. Drowning in scheduling? Start with Jobber. Pick the one pain point that costs you the most time.

    Use the free tier first

    Almost every tool here has a free trial or free tier. Test it on a real project before committing. If it doesn't save you time in the first week, move on.

  • 3

    Stack tools that integrate

    Jobber connects to QuickBooks. CompanyCam connects to Buildertrend. Choose tools that talk to each other so you're not re-entering data.
